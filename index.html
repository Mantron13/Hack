What we do
ChillBill is a user friendly anti depression web portal which inspires to dispel unhappiness and reinstate a positive mindset.It would aid distressed students by connecting them to nearby counselors who are qualified to tackle such situations.One of the main sources of comfort comes from the fact that a person is not alone in facing a situation, thus a blog in which students can share their struggles and redemption would be established in the portal in order for it to serve as a beacon of light for other distressed students.
